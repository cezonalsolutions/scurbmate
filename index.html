About Us

About Scrub Mate

Scrub Mate is a convenient cleaning and household assistance service designed to help customers book trusted service partners for everyday home and local cleaning needs.

1. What is Scrub Mate?

Scrub Mate is an online service-booking platform that connects customers with service partners for cleaning and household assistance. Customers can select a service, choose their location, make a booking, and receive service at their selected address.

2. Our Purpose

Our purpose is to make household cleaning and assistance services easier to access, especially for customers living in towns, villages, and nearby local areas.

3. Easy Service Booking

Customers can use Scrub Mate to choose the required service, provide their address and location, select a suitable booking time, and complete payment using the available payment methods.

4. Services We Provide

Scrub Mate may provide services such as home cleaning, bedroom cleaning, bathroom cleaning, kitchen assistance, window cleaning, fan cleaning, AC cleaning, fridge cleaning, dusting and wiping, laundry assistance, ironing, packing and unpacking, bike washing, shop cleaning, stage cleaning, and other household support services.

5. Local Service Partners

We work with available service partners from nearby operational areas. The assigned service partner receives the necessary booking details required to reach the customer and complete the selected service.

6. Service Availability

Service availability may depend on the customer's location, selected service, booking time, service partner availability, and operational conditions.

7. Our Commitment

We aim to provide a simple booking experience, transparent service details, timely updates, dependable assistance, and respectful service for every customer.

8. Customer Convenience

Scrub Mate is designed to reduce the difficulty of searching for cleaning helpers. Customers can conveniently book available services directly through the application.

9. Service Quality

Service partners are expected to perform the selected work carefully, professionally, and respectfully. Customers are encouraged to report any service-related concern through Scrub Mate support.

10. Safe and Respectful Service

Customers and service partners must maintain a safe and respectful environment. Abusive behaviour, unsafe working conditions, fraudulent bookings, or misuse of the platform may result in service cancellation or account restrictions.

11. Payments

Customers may complete payments through the payment methods available in the application. Payment availability may differ depending on the selected service and operational area.

12. Our Vision

Our vision is to build a reliable local service network that creates convenient household support for customers and meaningful work opportunities for service partners.

13. Support

For booking help, payment concerns, service complaints, or other questions, customers can contact the Scrub Mate support team through the application.
